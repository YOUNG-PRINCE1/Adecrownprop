About Us

Our company was founded in 2010 with a vision to provide exceptional real estate services to our clients.

Over the years, we have been working to become one of the leading real estate companies in the country, with a team of experienced agents and a portfolio of properties that cater to diverse needs and preferences.

Our mission is to provide our clients with a stress-free and successful real estate experience, and we strive to achieve this by offering personalized services, professional expertise, and a commitment to excellence.

Our History

  • 2010: Founded by Prince Adesemowo Abdulwasiu Adeleke also known as (Adecrown) with a vision to provide exceptional real estate services.
  • 2012: Expanded our services to include property management and rental services.
  • 2015: Opened our first branch in Lagos.
  • 2024: Launched our website and online platform to enhance our services.
